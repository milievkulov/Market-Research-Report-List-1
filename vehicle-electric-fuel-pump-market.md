<p><p>The vehicle electric fuel pump market encompasses various types, including roller pumps, gear pumps, vane pumps, and others. Roller pumps utilize rotating rollers to create pressure, making them efficient for low-viscosity fuels. Gear pumps use interlocking gears for consistent fuel flow, providing reliability in performance. Vane pumps feature sliding vanes for adjustable flow rates, offering versatility. Other technologies may include diaphragm and peristaltic pumps, catering to specialized applications. Each type serves distinct automotive needs, balancing efficiency, reliability, and performance.</p></p>
