<p><p>High concentration ozone detectors utilize different technologies to measure ozone levels in various applications. UV absorption detectors operate by exposing ozone to ultraviolet light, with the absorption properties varying based on ozone concentration, allowing precise measurements. On the other hand, electrochemical detectors employ a chemical reaction that generates a current proportional to the ozone concentration, providing real-time readings. Both technologies are crucial for ensuring air quality and safety in industrial, environmental, and health-related contexts.</p></p>
