<p><p>The smart catheters market is segmented into single lumen and multi lumen types. Single lumen catheters feature one channel for fluid delivery or sampling, offering simplicity and ease of use. In contrast, multi lumen catheters have multiple channels, allowing simultaneous administration of different medications or therapies, enhancing functionality in complex medical scenarios. The choice between these types depends on the clinical requirements, with single lumen often used for straightforward procedures and multi lumen utilized in intricate treatments requiring more versatility.</p></p>
