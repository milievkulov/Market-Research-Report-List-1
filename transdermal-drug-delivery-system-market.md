<p><p>The Transdermal Drug Delivery System (TDDS) is an innovative approach designed to administer therapeutic agents through the skin for systemic effects. Additionally, ongoing research and development efforts are leading to the introduction of novel formulations and materials, further propelling market expansion.</p><p>The market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.8% during the forecast period. Key trends include the development of microneedle systems, smart patches with integrated biosensors, and the incorporation of biodegradable polymers.
