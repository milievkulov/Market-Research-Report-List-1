<p><p>The Ultra Fine Silicon Powder market primarily consists of three types: Densified Silica Fume, Semi Densified Silica Fume, and Undensified Silica Fume. Densified Silica Fume offers higher bulk density and better flow properties, making it suitable for concrete applications. Semi Densified Silica Fume balances density and performance, providing versatility for various industrial uses. Undensified Silica Fume, with lower density, is often used for applications requiring high reactivity and pozzolanic properties, such as in construction and chemical industries.</p></p>
