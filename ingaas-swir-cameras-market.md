<p><p>The InGaAs SWIR camera market comprises two primary types: SWIR area cameras and SWIR linear cameras. SWIR area cameras capture images over a wide field of view, making them suitable for applications like surveillance, machine vision, and robotics. In contrast, SWIR linear cameras acquire data linearly, ideal for high-speed scanning applications such as spectroscopy and automated inspection. Both types leverage indium gallium arsenide technology to detect shortwave infrared wavelengths, enhancing performance in various industrial and scientific fields.</p></p>
