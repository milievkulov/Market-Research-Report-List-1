<p><p>The Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) films market encompasses three primary types: Polyester TPU, Polyether TPU, and Polycaprolactone TPU. Polyester TPU is known for its excellent abrasion resistance and mechanical strength, making it suitable for industrial applications. Polyether TPU offers enhanced flexibility and chemical resistance, often used in applications needing durability in harsh environments. Polycaprolactone TPU combines low-temperature properties with elasticity, making it popular in medical and specialty applications. Each type caters to specific performance requirements across various industries.</p></p>
