<p><strong>The Portable Spectrophotometer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Beam Type</li><li>Double Beam Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The portable spectrophotometer market can be categorized into single beam and double beam types. Single beam spectrophotometers measure light intensity at a single wavelength, making them compact and cost-effective, ideal for simple applications. In contrast, double beam spectrophotometers split light into two paths—one for the sample and one for a reference. This design enhances accuracy by compensating for fluctuations in light source intensity, making double beam models suitable for more demanding analytical tasks in various industries.</p></p>
