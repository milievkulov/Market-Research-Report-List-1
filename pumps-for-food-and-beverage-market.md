<p><p>The food and beverage market employs two main types of pumps: centrifugal pumps and positive displacement pumps. Centrifugal pumps are ideal for low-viscosity liquids, using rotational energy to move fluids efficiently, often seen in processes like brewing and bottling. Positive displacement pumps, on the other hand, are suitable for higher-viscosity substances, moving fixed volumes of fluid with each cycle, making them ideal for applications such as paste or gel handling. Both types ensure hygienic and reliable fluid transfer in the industry.</p></p>
