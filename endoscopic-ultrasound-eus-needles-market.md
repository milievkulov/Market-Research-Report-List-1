<p><ul><li>Aspiration Needles (EUS-FNA)</li><li>Biopsy Needles (EUS-FNB)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Endoscopic Ultrasound (EUS) needles market primarily includes two types: Aspiration Needles (EUS-FNA) and Biopsy Needles (EUS-FNB). EUS-FNA is used for fine-needle aspiration, allowing healthcare professionals to obtain cytological samples from suspicious lesions or masses. In contrast, EUS-FNB provides larger tissue specimens for histopathological analysis, enabling more accurate diagnosis. Both needle types are essential in gastrointestinal and oncological procedures, facilitating minimally invasive interventions and improving patient outcomes while contributing to advancements in diagnostic techniques.</p></p>
