<p><p>The lighted mirrors market comprises primarily two types: plane mirrors and curved mirrors. Plane mirrors are flat surfaces that provide a true reflection, widely used in bathrooms and vanity areas, enhancing illumination for grooming tasks. Curved mirrors, which can be concave or convex, manipulate reflected light to create magnified or distorted images, offering unique functionalities such as wider fields of view or artistic effects. Both types cater to varying consumer preferences for aesthetics and practical use in residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
