<p><p>Engineering liability insurance encompasses various market types designed to protect professionals and firms in the engineering sector. Directors and Officers (D&O) insurance safeguards corporate leaders against legal claims arising from their management decisions, ensuring that they are protected against financial losses. Conversely, Errors and Omissions (E&O) insurance covers professionals against claims of negligence or inadequate performance in their services. Both types are crucial in mitigating risks and ensuring financial stability for engineering firms in an increasingly litigious environment.</p></p>
