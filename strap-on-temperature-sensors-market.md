<p><p>The strap-on temperature sensors market encompasses various types, primarily including resistance sensors and thermocouple sensors. Resistance sensors, such as RTDs (Resistance Temperature Detectors), utilize the predictable change in electrical resistance with temperature to provide accurate readings. In contrast, thermocouple sensors consist of two different metals joined at one end, generating a voltage that correlates with temperature differences. Both sensor types offer reliable solutions for industrial applications, ensuring efficient temperature monitoring and control across diverse environments.</p></p>
