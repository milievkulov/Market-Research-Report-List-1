<p><ul><li>Direct Acting</li><li>Pilot Operated</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical equipment solenoid valve market is primarily divided into two types: direct acting and pilot operated. Direct acting solenoid valves function with minimal pressure, achieving quick response and precise control for small flow applications. In contrast, pilot operated solenoid valves utilize a small pilot flow to control a larger main flow, making them ideal for higher pressure applications. Both types are essential for regulating fluid dynamics in medical devices, enhancing operational efficiency and patient safety.</p></p>
