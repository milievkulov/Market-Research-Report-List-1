<p><p>The ultrasound market is primarily divided into diagnostic and therapeutic segments. Diagnostic ultrasound encompasses imaging technologies like 2D, 3D, and Doppler ultrasound, used for medical assessments, prenatal monitoring, and organ examination. Therapeutic ultrasound, on the other hand, focuses on treatment applications, including physiotherapy, targeting tumors, and pain management, utilizing ultrasound waves to promote healing or destroy tissue. Both sectors contribute significantly to the healthcare industry's advancements, offering non-invasive techniques and improving patient outcomes across various medical specialties.</p></p>
