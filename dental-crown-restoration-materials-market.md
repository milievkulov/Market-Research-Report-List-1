<p><p>The dental crown restoration materials market primarily features two types: metal-ceramic crowns and all-ceramic dental crowns. Metal-ceramic crowns combine a metal base for strength with a ceramic overlay for aesthetic appeal, making them durable for posterior teeth. All-ceramic crowns, on the other hand, are composed entirely of ceramic materials, offering superior aesthetics and mimicking natural tooth appearance, making them ideal for visible areas. Both types cater to diverse patient needs, encompassing durability, aesthetics, and biocompatibility.</p></p>
