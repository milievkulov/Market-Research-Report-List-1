<p><ul><li>Dry</li><li>Wet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semi-automatic sandblasting machine market is divided into two main types: dry and wet sandblasting systems. Dry sandblasting utilizes compressed air to propel abrasive materials onto surfaces, making it suitable for harder materials and precision work. In contrast, wet sandblasting combines water with abrasive materials to reduce dust and increase surface finishing quality, making it ideal for projects where environmental considerations are important. Both types offer unique advantages based on the application and desired outcomes.</p></p>
