<p><ul><li>Hydrostatic Bearings</li><li>Hydrodynamic Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Load Support Low Friction Fluid Film Bearing market is categorized into two primary types: hydrostatic bearings and hydrodynamic bearings. Hydrostatic bearings utilize an external pressure source to maintain a fluid film between the surfaces, providing smooth operation under varying loads. In contrast, hydrodynamic bearings generate a fluid film through the motion of the components, relying on relative movement to create lift and reduce friction. Both types are essential in applications requiring low friction, high load capacity, and reliable performance.</p></p>
