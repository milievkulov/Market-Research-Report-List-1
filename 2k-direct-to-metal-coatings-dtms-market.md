<p><p>2K Direct-to-Metal Coatings (DTMs) are dual-component systems designed for direct application on metal surfaces, providing enhanced durability and corrosion resistance. The market can be divided into two main types: waterborne and solventborne. Waterborne DTMs utilize water as a solvent, offering lower VOC emissions and easier cleanup, making them environmentally friendly. In contrast, solventborne DTMs rely on organic solvents, typically providing superior adhesion and performance in harsh conditions, but often have higher VOC levels and more stringent regulatory considerations.</p></p>
