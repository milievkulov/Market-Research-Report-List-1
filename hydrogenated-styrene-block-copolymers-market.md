<p><p>Hydrogenated Styrene Block Copolymers (HSBC) encompass mainly Styrene Ethylene Propylene Styrene (SEPS) and Styrene Ethylene Butylene Styrene (SEBS). SEPS offers enhanced elasticity and flexibility, making it suitable for applications like adhesives, sealants, and automotive components. SEBS, on the other hand, provides superior tensile strength and UV resistance, ideal for use in medical devices, consumer goods, and roofing materials. Both types contribute to improved performance characteristics in various industries, driving their increasing adoption in the market.</p></p>
