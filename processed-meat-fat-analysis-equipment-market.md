<p><p>The processed meat fat analysis equipment market features advanced technologies such as DEXA (Dual-Energy X-ray Absorptiometry) and NIR (Near-Infrared) spectroscopy. DEXA technology provides precise measurements of fat content by differentiating between lean and fat tissues based on their X-ray absorption characteristics. In contrast, NIR technology offers rapid, non-destructive analysis of meat composition, utilizing light spectra to estimate fat content. Both methods enhance quality control in the meat industry, ensuring product consistency and meeting regulatory standards effectively.</p></p>
