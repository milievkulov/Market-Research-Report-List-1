<p><strong>The Magnetic Therapy Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Constant Energy Magnets</li><li>Pulsed Electro-Magnetic Field Energy</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic therapy devices are primarily categorized into two market types: constant energy magnets and pulsed electromagnetic field (PEMF) devices. Constant energy magnets provide a steady magnetic field intended for pain relief and healing by stimulating blood circulation and enhancing cellular function. In contrast, PEMF devices deliver varying magnetic fields that pulse at different frequencies to promote tissue regeneration and reduce inflammation. Both types of devices are used in alternative medicine for therapeutic purposes, catering to different user preferences and health needs.</p></p>
