<p><strong>The Braided Suture Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbable Braided Suture</li><li>Non-absorbable Braided Suture</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The braided suture market is categorized into absorbable and non-absorbable types. Absorbable braided sutures are designed to break down gradually in the body, making them ideal for internal suturing where long-term support isn't necessary. Non-absorbable braided sutures, on the other hand, remain intact for an extended period, suitable for external wounds or situations requiring durable support. Each type offers unique advantages based on the surgical procedure and healing needs, catering to diverse medical applications.</p></p>
