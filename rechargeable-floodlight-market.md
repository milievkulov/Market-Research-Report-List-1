<p><p>The rechargeable floodlight market is primarily categorized into four types based on lighting technology: LED, halogen, incandescent, and fluorescent lamps. LED lamps are energy-efficient and long-lasting, making them popular for outdoor use. Halogen lamps provide bright, white light with excellent color rendering but have shorter lifespans. Incandescent lamps offer a warm light but consume more energy and have a limited lifespan. Fluorescent lamps are energy-efficient, yet bulkier and less preferred for portable applications. Each type caters to specific user needs and preferences.</p></p>
