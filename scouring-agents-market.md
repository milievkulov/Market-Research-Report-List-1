<p><strong>The Scouring Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Anionic Scouring Agent</li><li>Cationic Scouring Agent</li><li>Nonionic Scouring Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The scouring agents market is categorized into three main types: anionic, cationic, and nonionic scouring agents. Anionic scouring agents, typically derived from fatty acids, effectively remove dirt and grease due to their negative charge. Cationic scouring agents, with a positive charge, are utilized for their antimicrobial properties and ability to bond with negatively charged surfaces. Nonionic scouring agents, lacking a charge, offer versatility and compatibility with various formulations, making them ideal for delicate fabrics and sensitive applications.</p></p>
