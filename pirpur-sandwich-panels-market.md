<p><p>PIR (Polyisocyanurate) and PUR (Polyurethane) sandwich panels are composite building materials used primarily for insulation in construction and manufacturing applications. These panels consist of a core made of PIR or PUR foam, sandwiched between two facing materials, typically metal sheets. Known for their excellent thermal insulation, lightweight nature, and enhanced energy efficiency, PIR/PUR sandwich panels are widely used in industrial buildings, cold storage facilities, and transport sectors.</p><p>The market for PIR/PUR sandwich panels is experiencing robust growth, driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient construction solutions and stringent building regulations focused on sustainability. The emphasis on reducing carbon footprints and enhancing energy conservation is pushing various industries to adopt these panels. In addition, technological advancements are leading to improved properties of PIR/PUR materials, enhancing their appeal across diverse applications.</p><p>Furthermore, the rising trend of modular construction and prefabricated buildings is facilitating the use of PIR/PUR sandwich panels, boosting their market presence.
