<p><p>The ovarian cancer therapeutics market comprises several key treatment types including PARP inhibitors, PD-L1 inhibitors, and angiogenesis inhibitors. PARP inhibitors target cancer cells with DNA repair deficiencies, enhancing treatment efficacy in BRCA-mutated tumors. PD-L1 inhibitors work by boosting the immune response against tumor cells, promoting immune system recognition. Angiogenesis inhibitors disrupt the formation of new blood vessels that tumors rely on for growth, effectively starving the cancer. Together, these therapies represent significant advancements in managing ovarian cancer.</p></p>
